Masenko (LV1)
Gohan's famous attack is a short-range energy wave that draws the enemy in upon hitting them. For zoning prowess, Gohan is able to launch the Masenko diagonally upwards. When in Unleashed Mode, the Masenko is changed to its former chargeble, electric projectile glory. When you charge this up to a certain level, it dizzies the opponent.
Soaring Dragon Strike (LV1)
This Super move has Gohan dashing across the screen at high speed and leaping up with an enormous uppercut as soon as he reaches the opponent, hitting multiple times and sending them flying high into the air. You can dash-cancel out of the Strike after it ends hitting the opponent.
Thunder Flurry Kick (LV1)
Aerial rapid kick that halts Gohan's momentum and hits multiple times. This replaces his Super Hurricane Kick from prior versions. It's able to suck in the opponent if they're near and moreso if they're hit once, they're pulled in. Good combo-ender. You're able to supercancel into it during either of his Hurricane Kick special moves as well as his Diving Kick Flurry.
Masenko (LV2)
Souped-up version which fires a larger spark, this one reaches across the screen. The Unleashed version likewise is souped-up.
Soaring Dragon Strike (LV2)
Souped-up with a faster dash and more hits and damage! Also comes with a big aura surrounding his arm so you know Gohan means business! When in Unleashed Mode, you can dash-cancel out of the Strike once it starts hitting the opponent.
Super Thunder Flurry Kick (LV2)
This boosted-up version of the Thunder Flurry Kick has Gohan traveling forward as he performs the rapid kick, and ending with a Bicycle Kick that causes a ground-bounce.
Gekiretsu Ranbu (LV3)
Gohan's Ultimate is a counter move. If he's hit during his special pose, Gohan counters the opponent and destroys him with the badass beatdown combo he performed against Majin Buu. In Unleashed mode, Gekiretsu Ranbu becomes a standalone offensive move (no longer a counter) which can hit at mid-distance and you're able to combo into it, even able to catch aerial opponents with the shockwave it produces at the start. Also in Unleashed mode, Gohan gains access to different finishers for Gekiretsu Ranbu, which are performed automatically depending on the Unleashed Bar level;
Lv1 - Hi-Ex Blast Fist.
Lv2 - Demon Tribe Beatdown.
Lv3 - Flash Kamehameha.
