Claw Swipe
The Saibamen's most basic attack; a claw swipe.
Ki Blast
Don't underestimate these little buggers; they are able to shoot Ki Blasts! This is their most annoying attack as they fire these blasts at you while some of their fellow Saibamen keep you occupied.
Headbutt
A lunging attack that knocks the opponent away.
Burrow and Sprout
Tricky little bastards, they can dig themselves into the ground and move through it quite efficiently. At any point, they can leap out of the ground with a spin attack, knocking the opponent away.
