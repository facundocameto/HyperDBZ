Dodonpa (LV1)
Tien's go-to quick super shot, he charges energy to his index finger, then brings it forward and shoots a fast-traveling energy beam from it.
Blinding Wings
Physical Super move in which Tien rises up into the air while frantically twisting around a multitude of times while punching with his 'wings', carrying the enemy up with him and knocking them away on the last hit.
Four Witches Machine Gun
Another physical Super move; Tien grows out his second set of arms and then unleashes a rapid volley of strikes while moving forward, ending with an uppercut that launches the enemy into the air.
Kikoho (LV1~2~3) (knocks down)
Tien's most potentially devastating power attack! After activating the move, Tien coils back and upwards a little bit, gathers his latent energy and shoots it out in a quick wave which travels diagonally down. If this wave touches the enemy, it pushes them down to the ground along its path. When the wave touches the ground, it explodes into an erupting pillar of energy! Even if the wave misses the enemy, they can still get hit by the pillar.

Once the first pillar has been lit, you can mash the Punch button to shoot out another, then another, and another! Each new pillar has the cost of an EX special. You can even fire a couple more after you've run out of Super meter but be wary; from this point onward it also takes a little chunk of your health away, more and more with each new pillar. If you aren't careful, doing one too many Kikohos could actually drain too much of your health and lead to your own demise!
Shin Dodonpa (LV2)
An upgraded beam, larger in size and causing a big explosion upon impact with the enemy. Chiaotzu can't touch this! This can be chained from the Lvl1 Dodonpa too.
Ungrounded (LV3)
Tien uses the Multi-form Technique, splitting into 4 individuals all shooting into one direction. If the enemy is hit by any of these Tiens, they're caught in a 4-way barrage of attacks ending with a heavy knockdown. When performed in Om Mode, a Finisher is added at the end.
Lv1 - Volley Ball Beatdown.
Lv2 - Dual Dodonpa Assault.
Lv3 - Shin Kikoho.
