Dodonpa (LV1)
Tien's go-to quick super shot, he charges energy to his index finger, then brings it forward and shoots a fast-traveling energy beam from it.
Four Witches Machine Gun (LV1)
Another physical Super move; Tien grows out his second set of arms and then unleashes a rapid volley of strikes while moving forward, ending with an uppercut that launches the opponent into the air.
Back Breaker
The starter attack busts out a heavy uppercut. If this hits the opponent succesfully they're launched straight into the air. Tien leaps up to chase them, grows two extra arms and grabs them in mid-air, then comes falling down breaking their back over his shoulders. You can cancel into this at any point during the rapid-punches of Four Witches Machine Gun.
Kikoho (LV1~2~3)
Tien's most potentially devastating power attack! After activating the move, Tien coils back and upwards a little bit, gathers his latent energy and shoots it out in a quick wave that travels diagonally down. If this wave touches the opponent, it pushes them down to the ground along its path. When the wave touches the ground, it explodes into an erupting pillar of energy! Even if the wave misses the opponent, they can still get hit by the erupting pillar.

Once the first pillar has been lit, you can mash the Punch button to shoot out another, then another, and another. Each new pillar has the cost of a LV1 Super. You can shoot up to four follow-up beams, but if Tenshinhan runs out of life before the opponent is KO'd, Tenshinhan will lose even if that final pillar would have KO'ed the opponent. If you aren't careful, doing one too many Kikohos could actually drain too much of your health and lead to your own demise!
Super Dodonpa (LV2)
An upgraded beam, larger in size and causing a big explosion upon impact with the opponent. Chiaotzu can't touch this! This can be cancelled into from the LV1 Dodonpa.
Ungrounded (LV3)
Tien uses the Multi-form Technique, splitting into 4 individuals all shooting into one direction. If the opponent is hit by any of these Tiens they're caught in a 4-way barrage of attacks ending with a heavy knockdown.
