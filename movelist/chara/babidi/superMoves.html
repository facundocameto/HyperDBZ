Pui Pui Blaster (LV1)
Pui Pui appears on-screen and immediately charges his Sniper Shot, then shoots the projectile straight ahead. It explodes on impact.
Yakon Slasher (LV1)
Yakon appears onscreen and slashes 3 fiery hits, back and forth with his arm blade.
Dabura Blaster (LV1) (burns)
An underworld demon ruler, the great Dabura, is summoned by Babidi, right above him. Dabura then fires four firey projectiles downward. If he hits with one of the blasts, the opponent will be engulfed in fire for additional hits.
Dabura Hellfire (LV2) (burns)
Once more the demon lord is summoned, this time he performs a firey overhead fly-by assault. Babidi will automatically shield himself from the fireballs.
Super Enchanter (LV3)
Bibidi Babidi Buu! Our wizard lord shoots an energy orb around the stage which will bounce from the walls. If the ball hits the enemy, they will be trapped by the wizard's powerful chants and repeatedly hit by a variety of his elemental attacks.
