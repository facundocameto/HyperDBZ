Finger Blaster
Golden Freeza can fire a short 3-round burst from his finger with any Strong Punch button press, whether he's standing, crouching or jumping.
Kick Launch
This flipping kick launches the opponent into the air, then by pressing Up Freeza can chase after them.
Surperior Sky Dance
Unlike most other characters, Golden Freeza is able to perform two Strong Attacks in a single jump, be it two Strong Punches, two Strong Kicks or one and then the other. This also includes his Dive Kick (detailed below).
Dive Kick
Nice, simple and effective.
Neo Death Beam
The Neo Death Beam is a more versatile version of his famous move Instead of a straight beam, Golden Freeza is able to bend this beam, targeting a much larger area.

LP shoots straight like a common Death Beam, MP makes it bend and curve upwards pretty wide (good for hitting opponents jumping across the screen) and SP makes it curve even harsher (excellent to catch opponents jumping or falling above you). You can chain two of these together in any order you like (the follow-up commands are merely forward+Punch). However trying it a third time makes a unique version come out, a Death Beam that Freeza shoots behind him which then arcs all the way around and hits the opponent in the back!

The EX version sees Freeza firing off a frantic volley of Death Beams, launching the opponent up. This volley can even sweep an opponent off the ground!
Death Slicer
A toned-down version of Freeza's Telekinetic Air Cut, he swipes in front of him and a vaccuum cut appears. One hit and the opponent is knocked down. The EX version creates a traveling version of this wave cut which hits multiple times.
Crown Shatterer
An aerial grab. Freeza attempts to grab the opponent and when he succeeds, spins them around like a washing machine as he drives them towards the ground on which they're then slammed hard.
Hell Flame
Having harmonized the energy within his body, Freeza can conjure up flames which give access to unique new moves. There are two types: Red Flames which represent his strength and ferocity (Intense), and Blue Flames which represent his cunning and telekinetic abilities (Delicate). During idling poses, these flames can be seen on Golden Freeza. You can combine Red with Blue, stacking up for a maximum of 2 Reds and 2 Blues.

QCB+LK produces a single Red Flame. Repeating this action generates a souped-up Red Flame.
QCB+MK produces a single Blue Flame. Repeating this action generates a souped-up Blue Flame.
QCB+SK makes Freeza perform the new move which corresponds to his flame stock count. Let's walk through the selection of these:
1 Red Flame = Dual Red Disc. One hits the opponent in front, the other one loops back and hits from behind.
2 Red Flames = Dual Red Eye Beams. Hits multiple times and carries the opponent upward.
1 Blue Flame = Telekinetic Slam. Grabs the opponent from afar, carries them up and then drops them.
2 Blue Flames = Rock Shield. Freeza surrounds himself with rocks which explode if they touch the opponent.
1 Red Flame + 1 Blue Flame = Deadly Cage. He teleports behind the opponent to grab them, traps them in an energy cage which rides them against the wall and explodes.
2 Red Flames + 1 Blue Flame = Destroyer Wave. Basically a large Bakuhatsuha with more horizontal range.
1 Red Flame + 2 Blue Flames = Terrifying Tornado. Sucks in the opponent and knocks them into the air.
2 Red Flames + 2 Blue Flames = Big Ball Supernova. Chargeble for stronger versions (hold Strong Kick then release). Freeza launches a huge energy ball forward.
