Deadly Psycho Bomb (LV1)
Golden Freeza grabs the opponent using telekineses, chokes them, then causes a large explosion which rocks their socks off! Be aware that you can only grab an opponent that is airborne, be it jumping or falling.
Aerial Death Beam Raid (LV1)
Shot diagonally down from the air, Freeza fires off a volley of straight Death Beams.
Nova Crash (LV1)
No iffs or butts; Freeza powers-up then charges forward, crashing into the opponent hard. You can supercancel out of the landing of this attack.
Spider Death Beam (LV2)
A Death Beam is fired out of each of Freeza's fingers (minus his thumbs), creating a spider web of sorts. Once the opponent is hit by this, another series of Death Beams rain down on them from various directions.
Die By My Hand (LV2)
Golden Freeza fires a devastating energy beam diagonally upwards. If the opponent is caught by this, they get pummeled by a boat-load more hits as the beam keeps dealing them.
If I Can't Win, I'll Destroy Everything! (LV3)
Freeza channels all of his power through the ground which then erups as a magmafied pillar a bit in front of him. The opponent is carried up by this if they hit it.
