Activating Grudeless Mode makes Golden Freeza a bit more dangerous and ferocious!
-While you have a Red Flame active, the damage of every non-flame Special and every Super is increased with 10%. It increases to 20% if you have two Red Flames.
-While you have a Blue Flame active, connecting with any non-flame Special and every Super eats away the opponent's Super Bar. The amount increases with two Blue Flames.
-The "If I Can't Win, I'll Destroy Everything!" LV3 move now only costs LV2 to perform and adds Finishers at the end of it depending on what Grudgeless level you are:
LV1 - Death Beam Storm.
LV2 - Absolute Telekinesis Delight.
LV3 - Terrific Monkey Team-Up.
