Itano Circus (LV1)
A barrage of missiles is fired from the mech after it awesomely transforms into a standing tank-configuration.
You Know The Drill (LV1)
The great Pilaf mechanoid can even turn into a speedy car outfitted with a spinning drill on its front end! Ram into the opponent to poke some holes into them! You can press jump repeatedly to make the car hop.
Spike Louder Than Words (LV1)
Another sharp-object attack, here Pilaf controls a row of spikes installed beneath the ground before the fight! With the tap of a button of his remote, the spikes pop out in rapid succession, making it tough for the opponent to keep their footing! He makes three rows appear, one after another.
Grab Grabber (LV2)
You think you've seen all of the Pilaf posse's tricks, but here's one more! A grabber arm descends from the skies which then moves forward and backward across the screen. On its own it's harmless, but when you press Start, the arm lunges down! If this touches the opponent, they are grabbed and held in place for a while, creating an excellent oppertunity to initiate a tickle torture! You can attack with the arm as many times as you want, but the Super will end after the arm attack has either been blocked three times or has succeeded three times. A whiff with the arm grab doesn't count as an attempt.
The One Mighty Goo (LV2)
From the air, Pilaf fires a large smelly ball of ooze diagonally down to the ground. If it whiffs, that's bad luck. However if it hits the opponent, the goo surrounds them and they'll be locked in place for a few seconds, giving you open range to go ham and do some damage!
Pilaf Calamity! (LV3)
This Super starts off with a barrage of freezing ice lasers being fired. If these successfully hit the opponent, they're given the rowdy Pilaf Gang treatment!
