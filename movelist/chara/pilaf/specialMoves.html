Crabby Demeanor
Simply dashing forward on the ground results in this wicked running movement. Pressing any Punch button during it will perform a pinching attack, while any Kick button performs a slam.
Afterburning Sensation
Holding MK for a bit results in a thruster flame that lasts longer and hits more times.
Great Laser
Crouching MK fires a laser blast diagonally down to the ground. Even if it misses, the laser's impact on the ground causes some splash damage. Secondly, holding MK charges it up and makes the laser ricochet off the ground, firing it diagonally up into the air.
Noggin' Bruiser
Holding the Strong Punch for a bit turns the mech's fist into a mace, covering a tad more range and doing more damage.
Shu Cannon
QCF+P Deploys the ninja doggo Shu! Keep the Punch button pressed in to delay the attack. Releasing it fires Shu forward from the arm cannon. If he connects with the opponent, he'll drive-by-slash them. The EX version can't be held and Shu is fired immediately. His sword slash will do three hits though.
Mai Gunner
Heavy Mai brings out the artillery, a freakin' minigun which she fires while standing on top of the mech. As she's firing, you can freely move around, but performing an attack will cancel her firing attack. The EX version has Mai throwing out a duo of big bombs!
Fast Ball
A simply dashing attack. You're gonna want to use the follow-up by pressing the Kick button again upon hitting the enemy. The EX version doesn't allow for the follow-up, however the initial attack launches the opponent on impact.
Bouncing Ball
When doing the Fast Ball in mid-air, you get this attack. Upon hitting, the mech bounces off and you can repeat the attack. Isn't it fun repeatedly smashing the mech into the enemy's body?
Crabby Cross
A short dash into a double uppercut attack that launches the opponent a bit. The EX version allows you to cancel into other attacks immediately after hitting the enemy with the uppercut.
Vacuum Hold
The mech's built-in vacuum is put in use to pull the opponent closer. If they get close enough, the mech will grab them and then slam then down. The EX version slams them on the ground twice.
Accidental Discharge
There's a lot of buttons on that control panel... this is what happens when you accidentally press the wrong one! Who designed this mech to blow itself up anyway? This is a good move to...ehmm...use when....err...
