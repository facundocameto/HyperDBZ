Photon Bomb (LV1)
Replacing the Galick Gun is the Photon Bomb; a red energy sphere that breaks into a series of exlosions upon impact, juggling the opponent around. It hits OTG.

Machine Gun Tantrum (LV1)
This move was changed a bit; Instead of being an automated volley of 12 Ki Blasts, QCB,f+P is now solely an activation that gives Vegeta a stock of 'ammunition' for 12 Ki Blasts, which can be fired at any time (either while standing or while airborne) with just forward+Strong Punch. Though he will always shoot them in a volley of three at minimum. Simply keep holding the Strong Punch button to continue firing and discharge the entire 'ammo clip'.
Atomic Wave (LV1)
This move is actually a super grab that needs the enemy to be close in order to work to it's full potential. Vegeta sticks out his arm, taunting the enemy. If he touches the enemy he instantly launches an Atomic Wave which pushes the opponent all the way back to the wall. Upon hitting the wall, the wave explodes and the opponent is stunned for a moment. Should you whiff the initial gesture, it acts like a Super Taunt of sorts, though this leaves you completely wide open.
Royal Edict (LV1)
Laying the smackdown! After three punches, the third being a big uppercut that launches the enemy, Vegeta leaps up and delivers a kick to the opponent's exposed spine, keeping them stunned and floating in the air for a while, before finishing with a teleporting smash-down kick. The attack will end if the first punch whiffs or is blocked.
Big Bang Attack (LV1~2~3)
The Big Bang is a very slow moving projectile that can absorb lots of enemy projectile hits. So while you might not be able to quickly punish enemies from afar, its absorbing nature makes it act like a moving wall of death, though the opponent can jump over it. Also, you can keep charging the move for as long as you'd desire, making the Big Bang sphere grow in size up to four times. For about 2 seconds after Vegeta launches the Big Bang, you can input the RDP+P command to have him manually detonate the sphere. In later updates to Majin Vegeta the Big Bang was recoded to start out as a LV1 move but with the player able to charge it up by gradually draining any remaining power bar you currently have left, with a full power bar even able to boost it up to a LV3 move.
Saiyan Pride Beatdown (LV3)
A LV3 Super throw. When succesful, Majin Vegeta fires an Atomic Bullet. which send the opponent flying back against the wall and he'll then fire a short burst of Atomic Bullets that pin the opponent against the wall. Vegeta then automatically dashes towards them delivers a heavy shot to the gut. The "mash buttons" icons appear, so you know what to do...mash buttons for more attacks which equals more damage! At the end, he'll take a leap backwards and finishes it off with an Atomic Blast which explodes upon impact.
