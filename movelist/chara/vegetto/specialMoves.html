Launcher
Main tool for launching enemies into the air after which you can pursue them with an aircombo.
Stomp Kick
Flies straight towards the ground attempting to hit the enemy's cranium.
Dash Kick
Get kicked by Vegetto! For real!
Candy Thrust
An aura-charged forward flying punch attack. EX version is bigger and does more damage.
Going Underground
Vegetto loops around and digs himself underground where he can't get hit, able to burrow his way to where the enemy stands and then lunges out of the ground, knocking them upwards. Upon being underground, you can use left and right to burrow yourself into that direction. The LP version takes the shortest for Vegetto to shoot back out of the ground, while the SP version takes the longest. The EX version cannot be manually burrowed, but instead Vegetto will track the enemy's position.
Candy Pool
From the air, Vegetto aura-dashes downwards and upon nearing the ground, turns himself around and aura-dashes once more. The EX version adds one more direction-change and aura-dash.
