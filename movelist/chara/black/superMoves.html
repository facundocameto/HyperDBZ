Blade Storm (LV1)
Black launches two large volleys of piercing energy blades into the opponent, then gestures all of the blades to explode, launching his prey into the air.
Punishment (LV1~LV2)
Upon connecting with a forward dash, Black bursts into a Zamasu-assisted punching and kicking volley. If at any time during this volley you have another LV1 in stock and press 2 Punches, Zamasu will grab-hold of the opponent while Black charges up a Kamehameha, launching it head-on into his helpless victim.
Dark Omen (LV1)
Black teams up with Zamasu to launch a slow-moving energy ball towards the opponent, impending doom-style.
Holy Severance (LV2)
Black forcefully opens a dimentional rift (which can hit the opponent) from which a small army of Black clones emergy and then attack the opponent in a slashing cloud of death. Depending on the Punch button strength used, the clone army comes dashing in at different timings: with LP+MP they come out almost immediately, while with LP+SP it takes the longest. They'll always appear from behind Black, so even after you've switched sides with the opponent after creating the rift, they'll come from that side you're on.
Divine Cross (LV3)
Black teams up with Zamasu once more for this triple leaping slash attack.
