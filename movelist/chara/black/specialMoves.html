Double Jump
Black can double-jump! Even after having connected with an air-to-air or jump-in attack, immediately after that you are still able to perform that second jump, and also attack out of it again. Should give you plenty of options to play around with the opponent and create unique combos.
Neutral Air Attacks
With the exception of his Light Punch and Light Kick, all of Black's neutral jumping attacks have different animations compared to his backwards/forwards jumping versions.
Backdash Attack
Black, like a few other characters, can perform one of his jumping attacks while backdashing.
Hop Kick
Going either forward or backward, Black can perform a hopping kick.
Twin Kick
A double whammy kick, the second kick smashes the opponent towards the ground.
Tornado Kick
A two-in-one attack. After a low spinning roundhouse kick, Black automatically follows up with a high spinning roundhouse kick whilst traveling forward.
Passion Kick
If you catch a jumping or falling opponent with this leaping kick, Black rides them like a surfboard until they crash into the wall. Surf's Up!
Omen
Black's projectile attack. He swipes his arm, launching a quick volley of energy spikes which pierce the opponent and push them up against the wall, after which the spikes explode. The EX version creates a slow-moving multi-hitting energy wave instead.
Noose
Noose is a move which teleports Black into the air, twirling his energy scythe for a while as long as you hold the Punch button, then lunges straight down to the ground with a nasty overhead strike with the scythe, even creating a traveling energy wave on the ground. The LP version teleports Black to the rear of the screen, MP in the middle and SP on the far side, possibly switching sides with the opponent. The EX version is a different attack alltogether, a homing-teleporting excecution attack. After grabbing the opponent's head, Black slashes down through them. Keep in mind this attack won't be as succesful when performed as the opponent is jumping or falling.
En Garde
Engulfing himself in energy, Black dashes forward while stabbing repeatedly with energy blades. He'll travel farther forward and perform more slashes the stronger the Kick button used to activate the attack. The EX version travels the farthest and stabs the most. With nifty finger work you can supercancel out of this.
Zamasu Dive Kick
Zamasu is here to assist his Goku-bodied self! Comparable to SSJ Goku's Teleport Bar, Black comes with his own buffer bar which can hold up to two assist attacks. Performing one takes away a chunk of this bar and then slowly begins to refil. A physical attack to the opponent's head! They are launched up after being hit by this diving kick.
Zamasu Blaster
Zamasu fires three fireballs diagonally down in rapid succession, good for pressure.
Zamasu Stabber
The most basic of Zamasu helper attacks, he performs a forward sliding energy blade stabbing attack.
Zamasu Energy Palm
Appearing behind the opponent, Zamasu smashes them in the back with an energy-charged palm strike, knocking them towards Black.
